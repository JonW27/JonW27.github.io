Stringify ME!

Turn potentially vulnerable code into harmless entities in your HTML content strings


Feeling lazy? Writing up a huge bunch of documentation on your HTML5 semantic code? Stringify ME! converts all your < and > tags to &lt; and &gt; entities to save you time.

Suddenly writing 4 characters becomes 1 character! Say you just have the standard HTML5 boilerplate tags- thats about 80 characters to type with entities → Now only 20 characters!
