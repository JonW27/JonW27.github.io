Spec. H.S. Unseen Cons

with the occasional pro

By Jonathan Wong Fri 3 Oct. 2014

These are the cons of mostly specialized High Schools with the occasional other high school added in.
Stuyvesant H.S.- Escalators break all the time. You start to feel it in your legs after walking at least 30 floors. There are also is not as much communication between the administration and the parents and students.
QHS @ York- They have a filler class called Freshman Skills and do not have lockers. Clubs are only on Fridays and last for an hour only.
